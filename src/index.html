How It Works

The process of selling your car with Cardeal is easy and straightforward. Our employees will keep you informed so you will understand what happens next.


  • Fill out a form that provides accurate information about your car (i.e., VIN, year, make, model, mileage, condition of car, accidents

  • Get an instant offer on your car that is good for 7 days.

  • Provide access to car. Deliver car to closest cardeal location or have us come get it.

  • Provide title proving your ownership of the vehicle.

  • Cardeal will inspect your car to verify its condition.

  • If everything checks out, you can decide if you want to accept the offer. You can go with cash or apply the money to a trade in.
